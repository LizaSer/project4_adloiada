Our City-Holon

Children's city

Holon Billing itself as a "children's city," Holon is home to the Holon Children's Museum and the Mediatheque youth.
Holon plays host each year to a street carnival in celebration of the Jewish holiday of Purim, the Adloyada. Thousands of children dress up in costumes and the streets close down for a parade featuring colorful floats. theater.

Adloyada

"Until one no longer knows"

Adloyada is a humorous procession held in Israel on the Jewish holiday of Purim.
The name is derived from the rabbinic saying in the Talmud that one should revel on Purim by drinking "until one no longer knows" the difference between "blessed be Mordecai" and "cursed be Haman".
